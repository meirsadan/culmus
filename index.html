The Interactive Broad Nib Pen. This website demonstrates writing casually formal modern Hebrew letters. Click the arrows on the sides of the screen to move between letters. Drag your mouse or move your finger across the letter to pause the demonstration at different points. The pen and its angle are noted in red while the letter is being drawn. Click the letter on the bottom left of the screen to switch directions (between right/left-handed writing methods). This website was created by Meir Sadan and Daniel Grumer as an instructional aid for the Department of Visual Communication, Bezalel.
